Tomato Tarte Tatin

A savoury riff, perfect for late-summer tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 500 g tomatoes
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • salt to taste

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 C.
  2. Caramelise the sugar in an oven-proof skillet.
  3. Arrange the tomatoes cut-side down, season.
  4. Drape the puff pastry over the top, tuck in.
  5. Bake until golden, then invert onto a plate.
