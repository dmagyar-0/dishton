ZINGY LIME CHEESECAKE 🍋
[whole food, gf]

when dads tree is overflowing, lime is of cause the star of the show

Lime Curd
• 1 egg
• 1/3 cup lime juice (3 limes)
• honey 1-2 tbsp
• 1 tsp butter
• lime zest

Base
• 1.5 cups almond meal
• 1/4 cup honey
• 2 tbsp melted coconut oil
• lime zest

Filling
• 2 cups Greek yoghurt
• 1 - 1.5 tbsp gelatine (more = firmer set)
• 2 tbsp water
• 1/4 cup honey
• 1/4 cup lime juice (2-2.5 limes)
• lime zest
• [optional] a handful of blueberries

method

Lime Curd
* whisk the egg, honey & lime juice in a small pot over a low heat
* continuously whisk (to avoid scrambled egg!) until it thickens
* add in butter & lime zest
* remove from the heat & allow to cool

Cheesecake
* combine the base ingredients, pressing firmly into a cake tin [place in the fridge]
* whisk together gelatine & 2 tbsp water, set aside to bloom
* mix together the yoghurt & honey
* gently heat 1/4 cup lime juice with the gelatine until fully dissolved [don't let it get too hot]
* slowly pour the lime/gelatine mixture into the yoghurt, while whisking to avoid clumps
* pour over the base & top with blueberries
* spread the lime curd over the top, evening out
* allow to set in the fridge for at least 4 hours

ENJOY <3
