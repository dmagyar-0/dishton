The BEST veggie lasagna 🍝 layers on layers of comfort. Save this one! 👇

Serves 6

For the ragù:
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 2 x 400g tins chopped tomatoes
- 1-2 tbsp tomato purée
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- a handful of fresh basil

For the béchamel:
- 50g butter
- 50g plain flour
- 600ml milk
- a pinch of nutmeg
- 100g grated parmesan

To assemble:
- 250g dried lasagna sheets
- 125g mozzarella, torn

Method:
1. Heat the oil and soften the onion, carrot and garlic for 8-10 minutes.
2. Add the tomatoes, purée and oregano; simmer 20 minutes, then stir in the basil.
3. For the béchamel, melt the butter, stir in the flour, then whisk in the milk until thick. Season with nutmeg.
4. Layer ragù, sheets and béchamel in a dish, finishing with béchamel, parmesan and mozzarella on top.
5. Bake at 180°C for 35-40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
